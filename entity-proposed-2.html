A strong password is your front line of defense against someone impersonating you online. Additionally, you can help protect the sensitivity of company information by choosing a strong password.

As a reminder, good passwords:

  • Are at least 8 characters in length (longer is better)
  • Contain a mix of characters from each of these four groups: lower case characters, upper case characters, numbers, and non-alphabetic characters (e.g.: !@#$%^^&*()_-)
  • Are not based on words that appear in the dictionary
  • Do not contain information associated with you, such as your name or social security number
  • Are not re-used or used across more than one site
